About Us How it all began

What Makes Us Tick!

CloudReign Technologies is a company started with a vision to build and deliver custom domain solutions on the cloud. Our focus is to move organizations to the cloud and enable them to leverage the benefits of cloud computing. As part of our key initiatives, we train the users on Google Cloud based Suite of Applications which includes setting up a cloud environment. We have extensive knowledge building Chrome applications, have done numerous G Suite implementations, provided training on G Suite usage and how to effectively use G Suite’s complete range of applications. We also provide expert advice on how to use Android for Enterprise on the G Suite platform.

CloudReign is a Google partner and authorized reseller for all G Suite and Chrome products. We have more than 140 customers in both business and education verticals.


We have a team of experienced Google certified professionals, bringing years of expertise in Google Apps & Chrome. With experience of 100+ migrations we can provide flawless execution and end-to-end support both pre & post implementation & migration. We also provide expert training on Google solutions for the users and administrators on how to effectively use and administer GSuite and setup best practices for their organization. Google chrome devices are designed to provide enterprises and businesses with the ability to safely and securely manage devices remotely. CloudReign has extensive knowledge in this niche area and can provide excellent support for developing enterprise apps on the Chrome platform.
